About EvacuFinder PH

EvacuFinder PH helps you quickly locate the nearest evacuation centers across the Philippines during emergencies and natural disasters.

Features

  • GPS-based location tracking
  • Interactive map with 1,500+ centers
  • Filter by province and type
  • Mobile-friendly design
  • Get directions to any center

Data Source

Evacuation center data is sourced from OpenStreetMap (OSM). Please verify information with local authorities during actual emergencies.
